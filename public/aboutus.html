Elizabeth Fritz

Since 2012, Elizabeth has been involved with various design disciplines. Before returning home to her native NH, she was working in NYC where she worked on projects ranging from Upper East Side townhouses to landmarked hotel facades in both lighting and interior design. Prior to that, Elizabeth worked in media and government relations as a project manager for a non-profit in Boston. She received a double masters in Architectural Lighting and Interior design from Parsons the New School for Design. During her studies, she took part in the inaugural course of the Healthy Materials Lab which helped focus her interest in researching and specifying building products that were healthy for both humans and the environment.

Daniel Fritz

Daniel Fritz's interest in design and building began from helping his father build an earthship in Colorado. Currently he is working in restoration carpentry and stone wall building. He enjoys woodworking-- making bowls and picture frames for friends and family.
